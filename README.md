# Global Retail Executive Dashboard 📊

## 🎯 Business Objective
To provide retail executives with a high-level strategic overview of global sales, profit margins, and regional performance to identify growth opportunities and supply chain inefficiencies.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Power BI Desktop**
* **Power Query** (Data Cleaning & ETL)
* **DAX** (Advanced Measures & Time Intelligence)
* **Data Modeling** (Star Schema)

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Star Schema:** Connected Fact tables with dedicated Date and Product dimensions.
* **Interactive Dark-Mode UI:** Optimized for high-contrast visibility and speed-to-insight.
* **DAX Calculations:** Engineered measures for Profit Margin %, YoY Growth, and Regional Sales.
* **Geospatial Analysis:** Integrated map visuals for global revenue distribution.
